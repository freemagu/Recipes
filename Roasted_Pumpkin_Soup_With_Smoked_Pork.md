# Roasted Pumpkin Soup With Smoked Pork

> Seasonal hearty soup. More paleo than carnivore - good autumn recipe.

## Ingredients

- [ ] Pumpkin
- [ ] Smoked pork
- [ ] Bone broth or stock
- [ ] Onion or garlic (if used)
- [ ] Butter or animal fat
- [ ] Seasoning

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Roast pumpkin until soft and slightly caramelised.
- [ ] 2 — Cook smoked pork separately if needed.
- [ ] 3 — Blend pumpkin with bone broth until smooth.
- [ ] 4 — Add smoked pork.
- [ ] 5 — Adjust seasoning.
- [ ] 6 — Serve thick and hot.

## Notes

- More seasonal and paleo than carnivore.
- Increase smoked pork for higher protein.
