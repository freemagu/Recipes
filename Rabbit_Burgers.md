# Rabbit Burgers

> Lean, well-seasoned rabbit burger patties. Freezer-friendly batch.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷6) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 687          | 115              |
| Protein  | 130 g        | 22 g             |
| Fat      | 26 g         | 4 g              |
| Carbs    | 10 g         | 2 g              |
| Sugar    | 1 g          | 0 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 750 g minced rabbit
- [ ] 1½ tsp onion powder
- [ ] 1½ tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1½ tsp paprika
- [ ] ¾ tsp cumin
- [ ] 1½ tsp oregano
- [ ] 1½ tsp salt
- [ ] ¾ tsp black pepper
- [ ] 1 tbsp + ½ tsp avocado oil (or garlic mayo)
- [ ] 1 tbsp beaten egg (optional, only if mix is dry)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — In a large bowl, combine rabbit mince and all dry spices.
- [ ] 2 — Add avocado oil or garlic mayo.
- [ ] 3 — Mix gently until just combined. Add a little beaten egg only if the mixture doesn't bind.
- [ ] 4 — Divide into 6 equal balls and shape into patties (~1.5–2 cm thick).
- [ ] 5 — Chill in fridge for 10–15 minutes to firm up.
- [ ] 6 — Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Add a little oil.
- [ ] 7 — Cook burgers 3–4 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through (internal temp ~70°C).
- [ ] 8 — Rest for 2 minutes before serving.
- [ ] 9 — Serve with garlic mayo, avocado slices, or over salad/leaves/bun.

## Notes

- ~115 kcal per burger, with 21.6 g protein and minimal sugar.
