# Beef Stock (Instant Pot, No Bones)

> Rich beef stock from lean boiling beef - no bones, made in the Instant Pot.

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷4) |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 500 g Siedfleisch mager (boiling beef, lean)
- [ ] 600–700 ml water (just to cover meat)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Set Instant Pot to Sauté and brown the beef well on all sides (10–15 min).
- [ ] 2 — Add cold water to just cover the meat.
- [ ] 3 — Seal lid and cook on High Pressure for 90 minutes.
- [ ] 4 — Natural pressure release (15–20 min).
- [ ] 5 — Strain liquid into a jug.
- [ ] 6 — Reduce briefly if needed to reach ~400 ml.

## Notes

- Use as the base for cottage pie or any beef stock application.
