# Instant Pot Chicken + Stock

> Whole chicken and rich gelling stock in one Instant Pot run - a base for other recipes.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 whole chicken (1.2–1.8 kg)
- [ ] 2–4 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
- [ ] 1 tbsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp black pepper
- [ ] 1 onion (optional, omit if strict carnivore)
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves (optional)
- [ ] 1 bay leaf (optional)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Place whole chicken + thighs into Instant Pot.
- [ ] 2 — Add 1–1.5 litres water (enough to reach ~⅔ height of chicken).
- [ ] 3 — Add salt + pepper (and optional aromatics).
- [ ] 4 — Close lid, set to High Pressure for 25 minutes.
- [ ] 5 — Natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release.
- [ ] 6 — Remove chicken + thighs and let cool.
- [ ] 7 — Strain liquid — this is your rich stock for bolognese.
- [ ] 8 — Pull all meat from bones for your curry.
- [ ] 9 — Save the skin/fat/meat scraps if you want extra richness.

## Notes

- The stock will gel nicely when cooled — perfect for a carnivore diet.
- You can return carcass + skin to the Instant Pot and run another 30–60 min for an even stronger broth.
