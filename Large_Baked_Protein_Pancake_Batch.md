# Large Baked Protein Pancake Batch

> Meal prep breakfast - very high protein. Batch cooks for the week.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷6) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 1945         | 324              |
| Protein  | 167 g        | 28 g             |
| Fat      | 112 g        | 19 g             |
| Carbs    | 83 g         | 14 g             |
| Sugar    | 37 g         | 6 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 12 whole eggs
- [ ] Protein powder (vanilla or chocolate)
- [ ] Collagen powder
- [ ] Almond flour
- [ ] 2 bananas

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Blend all ingredients until smooth.
- [ ] 2 — Pour into a lined oven tray.
- [ ] 3 — Bake at ~160 °C for 15–20 minutes until set.
- [ ] 4 — Cool fully.
- [ ] 5 — Cut into portions and store in a vacuum-sealed container.

## Notes

- Very high protein.
- Higher fat from eggs and almond flour.
- Bananas add carbs and sugar.
- Slice into 6 portions for daily breakfast use.
