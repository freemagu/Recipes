# Popcorn Ice Cream with Caramel Swirls

> Popcorn-infused custard ice cream with caramel swirls.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷5) |

| Calories | 1970         | 394              |

## Ingredients

### Popcorn-Infused Cream

- [ ] 22 g unsalted butter
- [ ] 37.5 g popcorn kernels
- [ ] 375 ml whole milk (~385 g)
- [ ] 190 ml heavy cream (~200 g)
- [ ] 56 g granulated sugar
- [ ] 1.5 g fine sea salt (~1/4 tsp)

### Ice Cream Custard

- [ ] 2 large egg yolks (~34 g total)
- [ ] 20 g granulated sugar (extra)

### Caramel Swirl

- [ ] 90 ml caramel sauce (~120 g)

## Method

### 1. Pop the Popcorn (Stovetop)

- [ ] 1 — In a large saucepan with lid, melt 22 g butter over medium heat.
- [ ] 2 — Add a few popcorn kernels to test heat — once they pop, add the remaining kernels.
- [ ] 3 — Cover slightly ajar to allow steam to escape; shake occasionally.
- [ ] 4 — Once popping slows (2–3 seconds between pops), remove from heat and let sit covered for 1 min.
- [ ] 5 — Transfer popcorn to a bowl and sprinkle lightly with salt.

### 2. Infuse the Cream

- [ ] 1 — Return the popped popcorn to the pot with any remaining butter.
- [ ] 2 — Add milk, cream, sugar (56 g), and salt. Bring to a gentle simmer.
- [ ] 3 — Cover and let steep off the heat for 10 minutes.
- [ ] 4 — Strain through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a bowl, pressing to extract liquid. Discard solids.
- [ ] 5 — Chill in the refrigerator until fully cold.

### 3. Prepare the Custard Base

- [ ] 1 — In a mixing bowl, whisk together egg yolks and 20 g sugar until pale and creamy.
- [ ] 2 — Reheat the infused cream until warm (not boiling).
- [ ] 3 — Slowly whisk warm cream into egg yolk mixture to temper.
- [ ] 4 — Return to saucepan and cook on low, stirring constantly, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon (~82°C).
- [ ] 5 — Strain again if needed and cool. Chill thoroughly (3–4 hours or overnight).

### 4. Churn and Swirl

- [ ] 1 — Pour into your Gelato Chef 2000 and churn per instructions (about 30–45 min).
- [ ] 2 — When it reaches soft-serve stage, spoon in 90 ml (~120 g) caramel sauce and gently swirl with a spatula.
- [ ] 3 — Transfer to a container and freeze 2–4 hours until firm.

### 5. Optional Crunch Topping

- [ ] 1 — Add caramel-coated popcorn bits after churning or before serving for extra texture and visual fun.

## Notes

- Active prep & cooking: ~1 hour 15 minutes
- Chilling & freezing: ~6–8 hours total
- Yield: ~600 ml, creaminess 5/10
