# Apple Pie Ice Cream

> High-protein apple pie ice cream for the Ninja Creami, with real pie-crust flakes.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷4) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 1200         | 300              |

## Ingredients

### Apple Pie Filling

- [ ] 1 large apple (~150 g before peeling)
- [ ] 12 g butter
- [ ] 18 g brown sugar
- [ ] ½ tsp cinnamon
- [ ] ½ tsp lemon juice
- [ ] Pinch of salt

### Real Pie Crust (Flaky)

- [ ] 60 g plain flour
- [ ] 40 g cold butter
- [ ] 1 tsp sugar
- [ ] Pinch of salt
- [ ] 1–2 tbsp ice water

### Custard Base

- [ ] 2 egg yolks
- [ ] 180 ml heavy cream
- [ ] 200 ml whole milk
- [ ] 35 g allulose or erythritol
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] ½ tsp cinnamon
- [ ] Pinch of salt

### Texture Enhancers

- [ ] 20 g whey protein
- [ ] 10 g allulose or erythritol
- [ ] 0.8 g xanthan gum

## Method

### 1. Make Apple Pie Filling

- [ ] 1 — Peel and dice apple into 7–8 mm cubes.
- [ ] 2 — Melt 12 g butter in a pan.
- [ ] 3 — Add apple, 18 g brown sugar, cinnamon, lemon, and salt.
- [ ] 4 — Cook 6–8 minutes until softened and caramelised.
- [ ] 5 — Cool completely.

### 2. Make Real Pie Crust

- [ ] 1 — Mix flour, sugar, salt.
- [ ] 2 — Cut in 40 g cold butter until pea-sized lumps form.
- [ ] 3 — Add cold water gradually until dough forms.
- [ ] 4 — Chill dough 30 minutes.
- [ ] 5 — Roll to 3–4 mm thick.
- [ ] 6 — Bake at 190°C for 14–17 minutes until golden.
- [ ] 7 — Cool fully.
- [ ] 8 — Break into 5–8 mm flaky pieces.
- [ ] 9 — Freeze pieces 30 minutes before mix-in.

### 3. Make Custard Base

- [ ] 1 — Whisk yolks with 35 g sweetener + pinch salt.
- [ ] 2 — Heat cream + milk until steaming.
- [ ] 3 — Temper yolks, return to pot.
- [ ] 4 — Cook to 78–82°C until slightly thickened.
- [ ] 5 — Stir in vanilla + cinnamon.
- [ ] 6 — Cool 10 minutes.

### 4. Add Texture Enhancers

- [ ] 1 — Whisk whey protein, 10 g sweetener, and xanthan together.
- [ ] 2 — Blend into warm-cool custard for 60–90 seconds.

### 5. Add Apple for Flavour

- [ ] 1 — Blend in 40–50 g of the cooked apple filling.
- [ ] 2 — Leave remaining apple for mix-in.

### 6. Adjust Volume

- [ ] 1 — Ensure total volume is 680–700 ml.
- [ ] 2 — Top with milk only if necessary.

### 7. Freeze

- [ ] 1 — Pour into the 709 ml Deluxe container.
- [ ] 2 — Freeze 24 hours.

### 8. Spin

- [ ] 1 — Run Ice Cream cycle.
- [ ] 2 — Re-spin once or twice.

### 9. Mix-In

- [ ] 1 — Add remaining apple filling.
- [ ] 2 — Add 30–40 g frozen pie-crust flakes.
- [ ] 3 — Run Mix-In cycle.

## Notes

- Designed for the Ninja Creami Deluxe 709 ml pint.
- Sugar varies depending on brown sugar vs allulose choice.
