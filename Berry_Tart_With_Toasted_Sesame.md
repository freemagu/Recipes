# Berry Tart with Toasted Sesame

> Berry tart on an almond-flour base with a toasted-sesame layer and custard.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷9) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 2900         | 322              |
| Protein  | 80 g         | 9 g              |
| Fat      | 180 g        | 20 g             |
| Carbs    | 245 g        | 27 g             |
| Sugar    | 90 g         | 10 g             |

## Ingredients

### Base

- [ ] 165 g almond flour
- [ ] 110 g plain flour
- [ ] 90 g cold butter (cubed)
- [ ] 45 g erythritol or sugar
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] Pinch of salt

### Sesame Layer

- [ ] 40 g white sesame seeds (toasted)
- [ ] 20 g erythritol or sugar
- [ ] 30 ml milk or cream
- [ ] Pinch of salt
- [ ] ½ tsp tahini (optional)

### Filling

- [ ] 330 ml whole milk
- [ ] 3 egg yolks
- [ ] 33 g cornflour
- [ ] 30 g erythritol or sugar
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] 110 g Greek yogurt
- [ ] Zest of 1 lemon
- [ ] ½ tsp lemon juice (optional)

### Topping

- [ ] 180 g raspberries
- [ ] 130 g blueberries
- [ ] 130 g strawberries (halved)

### Glaze

- [ ] 2 tbsp apricot jam
- [ ] 1 tbsp water

## Method

### Base

- [ ] 1 — Mix almond flour, plain flour, salt.
- [ ] 2 — Rub in butter until crumb texture.
- [ ] 3 — Add egg + sweetener, form dough.
- [ ] 4 — Chill 30 mins.
- [ ] 5 — Roll thin, line 23 cm tin, prick base.
- [ ] 6 — Bake at 180°C for 18–20 mins until golden.
- [ ] 7 — Cool completely.

### Sesame Layer

- [ ] 1 — Toast sesame seeds in dry pan (3–5 mins, golden, nutty smell).
- [ ] 2 — Blend or crush seeds.
- [ ] 3 — Mix with sweetener, milk/cream, salt (plus optional tahini) into a smooth paste.
- [ ] 4 — Spread thin layer over cooled tart base.

### Custard

- [ ] 1 — Heat milk until steaming.
- [ ] 2 — Whisk yolks, sweetener, cornflour.
- [ ] 3 — Slowly add milk while whisking.
- [ ] 4 — Return to heat, stir until thick.
- [ ] 5 — Remove, add vanilla + lemon zest.
- [ ] 6 — Cool slightly, fold in Greek yogurt.
- [ ] 7 — Optional: stir in lemon juice once cooled.

### Assemble

- [ ] 1 — Spread custard over sesame layer.
- [ ] 2 — Arrange berries neatly (tight pattern).

### Glaze

- [ ] 1 — Warm jam + water.
- [ ] 2 — Lightly brush over berries.

### Finish

- [ ] 1 — Chill 1–2 hours.
- [ ] 2 — Serve slightly cool.

## Notes

- 23 cm tin, 8–10 servings.
