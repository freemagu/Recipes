# Zucchini Sandwich Buns

Low-carb zucchini sandwich buns made from grated zucchini, mozzarella, garlic powder, and salt. Each serving is 1 complete sandwich bun, made from 2 halves.

## Ingredients

- 6 large zucchini, approximately 1.2 kg before squeezing
- 4g salt
- 15g garlic powder
- 336g shredded mozzarella cheese
- Avocado oil spray, for the parchment paper

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 205°C / 400°F.
2. Grate the zucchini into a large bowl.
3. Place the grated zucchini in a clean tea towel and squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
4. Return the squeezed zucchini to the bowl.
5. Add the salt, garlic powder, and shredded mozzarella.
6. Mix thoroughly until evenly combined.
7. Line baking trays with parchment paper and lightly spray with avocado oil.
8. Divide the mixture into 12 equal flat rounds.
9. Flatten each round to an even thickness.
10. Bake for 20-22 minutes, until firm and lightly browned.
11. Allow to cool for several minutes before handling.
12. Pair 2 halves together to form 1 sandwich bun.
