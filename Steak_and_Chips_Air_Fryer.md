# Steak and Chips Air Fryer

> Simple high-protein meal with controlled carbs. Good training day meal.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷1) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 910          | 910              |
| Protein  | 55 g         | 55 g             |
| Fat      | 55 g         | 55 g             |
| Carbs    | 45 g         | 45 g             |
| Sugar    | 2 g          | 2 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] Steak
- [ ] Chips or potatoes
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Cooking fat (if used)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Cook chips first if they take longer (depends on cut).
- [ ] 2 — Rest / hold chips while steak cooks.
- [ ] 3 — Air fry steak to preferred doneness.
- [ ] 4 — Rest steak ~5 min before eating.

## Notes

- Good training day meal.
- Chips increase carbs and calories — fine on training days.
- Track cooking fat honestly.
