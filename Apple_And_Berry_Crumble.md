# Apple and Berry Crumble

> Individual apple-and-berry crumbles with a buttery pecan topping.

## Ingredients

### Fruit Filling

- [ ] 1 medium apple, peeled, cored, diced small (approx. 180 g)
- [ ] 160 g frozen mixed berries
- [ ] 1 tbsp sugar
- [ ] ¼ tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1 tsp lemon juice
- [ ] 1 tsp cornflour

### Crumble Topping

- [ ] 50 g plain wheat flour
- [ ] 30 g cold butter, cubed
- [ ] 25 g sugar (brown or white)
- [ ] 20 g pecans, roughly chopped
- [ ] Pinch of salt

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Preheat oven to 180°C (fan 160°C). Place ramekins on a baking tray.
- [ ] 2 — In a bowl, mix apple, frozen berries, sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice, and cornflour until evenly coated.
- [ ] 3 — Divide fruit mixture evenly between the two ramekins.
- [ ] 4 — In another bowl, combine flour, sugar, pecans, and salt.
- [ ] 5 — Rub in cold butter with fingertips until the mixture forms loose clumps.
- [ ] 6 — Sprinkle crumble topping evenly over the fruit. Do not press down.
- [ ] 7 — Bake for 30 to 35 minutes until golden and bubbling.
- [ ] 8 — Rest for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.

## Notes

- Makes 2 × 300 g ramekins.
