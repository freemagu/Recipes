# Healthy Egg Custard with Greek Yoghurt

> High-protein dessert made with fat-free Greek yoghurt instead of milk. Sweetened with monk fruit / erythritol so sugar stays near zero.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 4 eggs
- [ ] 200 g fat-free Greek yoghurt
- [ ] 30 g whey protein
- [ ] Monk fruit or erythritol sweetener (to taste)
- [ ] Cinnamon (on top)
- [ ] Vanilla (optional)

## Method

- [ ] 1 - Beat eggs in a bowl.
- [ ] 2 - Add fat-free Greek yoghurt and whisk until smooth.
- [ ] 3 - Add whey carefully and mix until fully combined.
- [ ] 4 - Add monk fruit or erythritol sweetener to taste.
- [ ] 5 - Pour into an oven dish.
- [ ] 6 - Sprinkle cinnamon on top.
- [ ] 7 - Bake gently at 150-160 C until edges are firm and the centre has a slight wobble.
- [ ] 8 - Cool before eating so it sets properly.

## Notes

- Gelatin is optional and not required for this version because the eggs, yoghurt and whey provide enough structure.
- Texture will be thicker and creamier than the milk version.
- The yoghurt gives a slight tang, closer to a baked cheesecake style custard.
- Do not stir once baking begins if setting as custard.
- Centre should not be fully liquid; a slight wobble is right.
- Cooling improves texture significantly.
