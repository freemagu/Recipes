# Rösti, Lardons, and Eggs

> High-calorie savoury meal - a treat or high-energy meal, not a strict cut meal.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷1) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 985          | 985              |
| Protein  | 30 g         | 30 g             |
| Fat      | 70 g         | 70 g             |
| Carbs    | 55 g         | 55 g             |
| Sugar    | 1 g          | 1 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 250 g rösti
- [ ] 75 g lardons
- [ ] 2 eggs

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Cook lardons first to render fat.
- [ ] 2 — Add rösti and cook until browned in the rendered fat.
- [ ] 3 — Add eggs separately or on top.
- [ ] 4 — Serve hot.

## Notes

- Higher salt from lardons and rösti.
- More of a treat or high-energy meal.
- Save for refeed days or higher-output days.
