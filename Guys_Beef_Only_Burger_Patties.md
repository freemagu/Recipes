# Guy's Beef-Only Burger Patties

> Seasoned beef-only burger patties - no liver, no egg. Freezer-friendly batch.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷9) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 2320         | 258              |
| Protein  | 154 g        | 17 g             |
| Fat      | 180 g        | 20 g             |
| Carbs    | 5 g          | 1 g              |
| Sugar    | 1 g          | 0 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 900 g ground beef
- [ ] 2 tsp rustic mustard
- [ ] 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 1½ tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 2 tsp onion powder
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] ½ tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1 tsp sweet paprika
- [ ] ½ tsp dried thyme
- [ ] ½ tsp dried oregano
- [ ] ¼ tsp chili flakes or cayenne (optional)
- [ ] 1½ tsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp black pepper (or sub with mild chili powder if needed)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
- [ ] 2 — Mix thoroughly by hand until fully integrated.
- [ ] 3 — Shape into 8–10 patties (~90–110 g each).
- [ ] 4 — Sear in a cast iron pan over medium-high heat (2–3 min per side).
- [ ] 5 — Optional: finish in oven at 180°C for 5–7 min (target internal temp: 70°C).

## Notes

- No liver, no egg.
- Freezer-friendly — freeze raw patties with baking paper in between.
