# Beef Taco Bowls

## Ingredients

* 1.2 kg beef mince (15% fat, raw weight)
* 1.2 kg sweet potato
* 30 g olive oil (2 tbsp)
* 1 large avocado (approximately 200 g edible flesh)
* Salsa (not counted in macros)

## Method

1. Peel and dice the sweet potatoes.
2. Toss with the olive oil and roast until tender and lightly browned.
3. Brown the beef mince in a large pan and season to taste.
4. Divide the cooked sweet potato and beef evenly between 6 containers.
5. Top each portion with approximately ⅙ of the avocado.
6. Add salsa when serving.
