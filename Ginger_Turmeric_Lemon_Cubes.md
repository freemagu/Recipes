# Ginger Turmeric Lemon Cubes

> Morning health drink. Anti-inflammatory ginger + turmeric, black pepper for absorption.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷20) |
| -------- | ------------ | ----------------- |
| Calories | 195          | 10                |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 50 g fresh ginger
- [ ] 50 g fresh turmeric
- [ ] 6 lemons (juiced)
- [ ] Black pepper seeds (small handful)
- [ ] Honey (small amount — not too sweet)
- [ ] Water if needed

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Blend ginger, turmeric, lemon juice, black pepper, and a small amount of honey.
- [ ] 2 — Add water only if needed to blend.
- [ ] 3 — Pour into ice cube trays.
- [ ] 4 — Freeze.
- [ ] 5 — Use 1 cube each morning with warm water.

## Notes

- Not super sweet — small honey only.
- Black pepper supports turmeric (curcumin) absorption.
- 1 cube per day with warm water.
