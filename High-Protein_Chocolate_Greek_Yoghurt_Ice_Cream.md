# High-Protein Chocolate Greek Yoghurt Ice Cream

> High-protein dessert. Replaces normal ice cream.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷2) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 430          | 215              |
| Protein  | 58 g         | 29 g             |
| Fat      | 12 g         | 6 g              |
| Carbs    | 26 g         | 13 g             |
| Sugar    | 15 g         | 8 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] ~300 g fat-free Greek yoghurt
- [ ] Chocolate protein powder
- [ ] Cocoa powder (if desired)
- [ ] Sweetener (monk fruit / erythritol)
- [ ] 85% dark chocolate, chopped (optional)
- [ ] Zero-sugar white chocolate chips (optional)
- [ ] Vanilla (optional)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Mix Greek yoghurt with chocolate protein powder.
- [ ] 2 — Add cocoa powder for stronger chocolate flavour.
- [ ] 3 — Add sweetener to taste.
- [ ] 4 — Stir in chopped dark chocolate or sugar-free chips.
- [ ] 5 — Freeze for ~2 hours.
- [ ] 6 — Stir during freezing for better texture if you want it scoopable.

## Notes

- Higher cocoa = stronger chocolate flavour.
- Dark chocolate adds fat and calories — track honestly.
- Good late-evening protein hit if you're under target.
