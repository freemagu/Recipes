# High-Protein Blueberry Pecan Baked Pancake (Cottage Cheese Version)

> Baked protein pancake with blueberries, pecans, Greek yoghurt, and cottage cheese.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 large banana
- [ ] 4 large eggs
- [ ] 43 g vanilla whey isolate protein powder
- [ ] 20 g egg white powder
- [ ] 65 g fat-free Greek yoghurt
- [ ] 100 g cottage cheese
- [ ] ~30 ml milk (splash)
- [ ] 100 g blueberries
- [ ] 30 g pecans
- [ ] Cinnamon (optional)
- [ ] Pinch of salt (optional)

## Method

- [ ] 1 - Preheat oven to 160°C.
- [ ] 2 - Add banana, eggs, whey isolate, egg white powder, Greek yoghurt, cottage cheese, and milk into a blender.
- [ ] 3 - Blend until smooth.
- [ ] 4 - Stir in chopped pecans.
- [ ] 5 - Pour mixture into a lightly greased baking dish.
- [ ] 6 - Bake for 8-10 minutes.
- [ ] 7 - Remove from oven and scatter blueberries on top.
- [ ] 8 - Return to oven and bake another 8-12 minutes until centre is just set.
- [ ] 9 - Rest for 5 minutes before serving.
