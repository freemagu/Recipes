# Coconut White Chocolate PB2 Cloud Bread

> Exceptionally lean: ~71 g protein for only 337 kcal.

## Ingredients

- 400 g liquid egg whites (whole carton)
- 20 g Vilgain White Chocolate Whey
- 10 g egg white powder
- 10 g PB2 powdered peanut butter
- 20 g Lakanto Monk Fruit sweetener
- 1 tsp coconut flavouring
- Pinch of salt

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 140°C.
2. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
3. Add egg whites and salt to a large bowl.
4. Whip until stiff peaks form.
5. Slowly add the Lakanto while continuing to whisk.
6. Mix together:
   - 20 g whey
   - 10 g egg white powder
   - 10 g PB2
7. Sift the dry ingredients over the egg white foam.
8. Fold in gently with a spatula until just combined.
9. Add coconut flavouring and fold briefly.
10. Shape into a loaf or dome on the baking paper.
11. Bake for 40-50 minutes.
12. Turn the oven off and leave inside for 15 minutes with the door slightly open.
13. Remove and cool completely before slicing.
