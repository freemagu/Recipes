# No-Bake Lemon–Lime Cheesecake with Berries

> No-bake lemon-lime cheesecake topped with fresh berries.

## Ingredients

### Base

- [ ] 200 g digestive biscuits (or graham crackers)
- [ ] 100 g unsalted butter, melted

### Filling

- [ ] 400 g cream cheese, softened
- [ ] 200 ml heavy cream
- [ ] 100 g sugar
- [ ] Juice of 1 medium lemon (~3 tbsp)
- [ ] Zest of 1 lemon
- [ ] Juice of 1 medium lime (~2 tbsp)
- [ ] Zest of 1 lime
- [ ] 2 tsp gelatin powder (or agar-agar)
- [ ] 3 tbsp cold water

### Garnish

- [ ] Fresh raspberries
- [ ] Fresh blueberries
- [ ] Fresh strawberries or blackberries (optional)
- [ ] Whipped cream (optional)
- [ ] Mint leaves (optional)

### Optional Berry Sauce

- [ ] 1 cup mixed berries
- [ ] 2 tbsp sugar
- [ ] 1 tsp lemon juice

## Method

### 1. Prepare the base

- [ ] 1 — Crush biscuits into fine crumbs.
- [ ] 2 — Mix with melted butter until sandy in texture.
- [ ] 3 — Press firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
- [ ] 4 — Chill in the fridge while preparing filling.

### 2. Bloom the gelatin

- [ ] 1 — Sprinkle gelatin over 3 tbsp cold water.
- [ ] 2 — Let it sit 5–10 minutes until spongy.

### 3. Make the filling

- [ ] 1 — Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
- [ ] 2 — Add lemon juice, lime juice, and both zests. Mix well.
- [ ] 3 — In another bowl, whip cream to soft peaks.
- [ ] 4 — Gently heat bloomed gelatin until melted.
- [ ] 5 — Stir melted gelatin into the cream cheese mixture.
- [ ] 6 — Fold whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until smooth.

### 4. Chill

- [ ] 1 — Pour filling over the biscuit base.
- [ ] 2 — Smooth the top with a spatula.
- [ ] 3 — Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).

### 5. Garnish & Serve

- [ ] 1 — Remove from springform pan carefully.
- [ ] 2 — Top with fresh raspberries, blueberries, and optional strawberries or blackberries.
- [ ] 3 — Add whipped cream and mint leaves if desired.
- [ ] 4 — Slice into 9 servings and enjoy.

### 6. Optional Berry Sauce

- [ ] 1 — Place 1 cup mixed berries, 2 tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp lemon juice in a small saucepan.
- [ ] 2 — Cook over medium heat until berries break down and sauce thickens slightly.
- [ ] 3 — Cool before drizzling over cheesecake slices.

## Notes

- 9 servings, 9-inch springform pan.
- Per-serving: ~270 kcal, 4 g protein, 20 g fat, 18 g carbs, 13 g sugar (whole-recipe totals reflected in frontmatter).
