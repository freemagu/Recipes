# Aki's Moroccan Chicken

> Slow-cooked Moroccan-style chicken with warm spices, preserved lemon, and chickpeas.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1.5 kg chicken pieces (bone-in preferred)
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] 1 large onion (tennis-ball sized), chopped
- [ ] 4–6 garlic cloves, chopped
- [ ] 1 large carrot or 2 small carrots, sliced or diced
- [ ] Fresh parsley, chopped
- [ ] Fresh coriander, chopped
- [ ] 2–3 tbsp preserved lemon (or half jar)
- [ ] Dried chickpeas (soaked overnight and cooked)

### Spice Mix (no salt)

- [ ] 1.5–2 tbsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1 tbsp turmeric
- [ ] 1 tbsp ground ginger
- [ ] 0.5 tsp ground cinnamon
- [ ] 0.25 tsp ground nutmeg
- [ ] 0.5 tsp ground coriander
- [ ] 0.5 tsp paprika

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Mix all spices in a bowl (no salt, as the preserved lemon is very salty).
- [ ] 2 — Rub the spice mix thoroughly into the chicken pieces.
- [ ] 3 — Rest for at least 15 minutes.
- [ ] 4 — Heat olive oil in a large heavy pan over medium heat.
- [ ] 5 — Brown the chicken pieces on all sides, then remove and set aside.
- [ ] 6 — In the same pan, gently fry the onion and carrot until soft (8–10 minutes).
- [ ] 7 — Add garlic and cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
- [ ] 8 — Return chicken to the pan and add the preserved lemon.
- [ ] 9 — Add enough water to just cover the chicken and vegetables.
- [ ] 10 — Bring to a gentle simmer, add half of the chopped herbs, then cover with a lid.
- [ ] 11 — Cook on very low heat for at least 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
- [ ] 12 — Adjust liquid if needed (sauce should be rich, not watery).
- [ ] 13 — Finish with remaining fresh herbs before serving.
- [ ] 14 — Serve hot with cooked chickpeas on the side or stirred into the sauce.

## Notes

- This dish improves after resting and tastes even better the next day.
- Optional: serve with rice or flatbread.
