# BBQ Black Tiger Prawns

> High-protein fast BBQ meal. Lean protein, great flavour.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷2) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 720          | 360              |
| Protein  | 72 g         | 36 g             |
| Fat      | 45 g         | 23 g             |
| Carbs    | 7 g          | 4 g              |
| Sugar    | 1 g          | 1 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] Black tiger prawns
- [ ] Garlic
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Olive oil or avocado oil
- [ ] Lemon or preserved lemon
- [ ] Optional herbs
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Pepper

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Defrost prawns fully.
- [ ] 2 — Mix garlic, melted butter, oil, lemon, and seasoning.
- [ ] 3 — Marinate prawns for up to 90 minutes.
- [ ] 4 — BBQ quickly over high heat.
- [ ] 5 — Do not overcook — prawns are done as soon as they curl and turn opaque.

## Notes

- Prawns cook fast — don't overcook.
- Butter improves flavour.
- Preserved lemon adds salt.
- Good lean dinner.
