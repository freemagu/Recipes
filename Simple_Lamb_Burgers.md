# Simple Lamb Burgers

Six lamb burgers made from 600g minced lamb. Cooked with a light spray of avocado oil.

## Ingredients

- 600g minced lamb
- Small amount of avocado oil spray for cooking

## Method

1. Divide 600g minced lamb into 6 equal portions of 100g each.
2. Form each portion into a burger patty.
3. Lightly spray the pan with avocado oil.
4. Fry the burgers until cooked to your preferred level of doneness.
5. Serve immediately.
