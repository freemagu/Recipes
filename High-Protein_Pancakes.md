# High-Protein Pancakes

> High-protein breakfast or meal prep. Good for feeding days or training days.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷1) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 1005         | 1005             |
| Protein  | 97 g         | 97 g             |
| Fat      | 44 g         | 44 g             |
| Carbs    | 59 g         | 59 g             |
| Sugar    | 37 g         | 37 g             |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 large banana
- [ ] 4 eggs
- [ ] ~43 g vanilla whey isolate
- [ ] 10 g egg white powder
- [ ] 12 g collagen powder
- [ ] 120 g fat-free Greek yoghurt
- [ ] Splash of milk
- [ ] ~100 g blueberries
- [ ] ~30 g pecans

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Blend banana and eggs.
- [ ] 2 — Add whey, egg white powder, collagen, Greek yoghurt, and milk. Mix until smooth.
- [ ] 3 — Cook as pancakes in a pan, or pour into a tray and bake.
- [ ] 4 — Add blueberries and pecans at the start or partway through depending on texture preference.
- [ ] 5 — Cook until set.

## Notes

- High protein but not carnivore.
- Banana and blueberries add carbs and sugar — track on macro day.
- Pecans add fat and calories.
