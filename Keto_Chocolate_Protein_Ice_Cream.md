# Keto Chocolate Protein Ice Cream (Butter Edition)

> Keto chocolate protein ice cream for the Ninja Creami, enriched with butter.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷2) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 565          | 283              |
| Protein  | 39 g         | 20 g             |
| Fat      | 36 g         | 18 g             |
| Carbs    | 22 g         | 11 g             |
| Sugar    | 5 g          | 3 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 350 ml full-fat raw milk
- [ ] 1 egg yolk (raw, pasture-raised preferred)
- [ ] 20 g unsalted melted butter
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] 30 g Sponsor Chocolate Protein Powder
- [ ] 10 g unsweetened cocoa powder
- [ ] 25 g erythritol (or allulose for smoother texture)
- [ ] ½ tsp xanthan gum (optional, for texture)
- [ ] 1 pinch salt

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Melt butter gently until liquid (do not overheat).
- [ ] 2 — Add raw milk, egg yolk, vanilla, and melted butter to a blender.
- [ ] 3 — Blend on high for 60–90 seconds until smooth and emulsified.
- [ ] 4 — Add protein powder, cocoa powder, erythritol, xanthan gum, and salt.
- [ ] 5 — Blend again for 60–90 seconds; let rest 5 min, then blend once more.
- [ ] 6 — Pour mixture into Ninja Creami pint (below MAX line).
- [ ] 7 — Freeze 24–36 hours until solid.
- [ ] 8 — Run outside of pint under warm water for 60 seconds.
- [ ] 9 — Process on "Light Ice Cream" or "Gelato" setting.
- [ ] 10 — Scrape sides, add 1–2 tbsp raw milk if needed, then Re-Spin once or twice.
- [ ] 11 — Serve immediately for soft-serve or refreeze 30 min for firmer texture.

## Notes

- For 0.7 L Ninja Creami.
- Fiber: ~16 g; net carbs: ~6 g.
- Use allulose for a creamier, less icy result.
- Add 1 tsp MCT oil for extra smoothness and keto energy.
- Chill mixture before freezing to keep butter emulsified.
- Serve immediately after Re-Spin — keto ice creams freeze firm quickly.
