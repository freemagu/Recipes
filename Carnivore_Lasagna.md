# Carnivore Lasagna

> Grain-free carnivore lasagna using baked cottage-cheese sheets instead of pasta.

## Nutrition

## Ingredients

### Pasta Sheets (makes 2 sheets)

- [ ] 450 g cottage cheese (2 cups)
- [ ] 4 large eggs
- [ ] 50 g grated Parmesan (½ cup) (optional)
- [ ] 1 tsp Italian seasoning (optional)
- [ ] ½ tsp black pepper (optional)

### Meat Sauce

- [ ] 450 g ground beef (1 lb)
- [ ] 340 g Italian sausage (¾ lb)
- [ ] 1 tbsp dried chopped onion
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tbsp tomato paste (optional)
- [ ] 1 tbsp hot sauce (optional)
- [ ] 120 ml beef broth (½ cup)

### Cheese Filling

- [ ] 450–680 g ricotta cheese (1–1½ lb)
- [ ] 1 tsp Italian seasoning (optional)
- [ ] ½ tsp black pepper (optional)

### For Layering

- [ ] 200–300 g shredded mozzarella or mozzarella/cheddar blend (2–3 cups)

## Method

### 1. Make the Pasta Slab

- [ ] 1 — Preheat oven to 190°C.
- [ ] 2 — Blend cottage cheese, eggs, Parmesan, and seasoning.
- [ ] 3 — Line 30 × 40 cm baking tray with parchment paper.
- [ ] 4 — Spread mixture ~5–6 mm thick.
- [ ] 5 — Bake 40 minutes.
- [ ] 6 — Cool 10–15 minutes.
- [ ] 7 — Cut into two sheets ~23 × 33 cm.

### 2. Cook the Meat Sauce

- [ ] 1 — Heat skillet over medium heat.
- [ ] 2 — Brown beef and sausage (8–10 minutes).
- [ ] 3 — Add onion, garlic powder, tomato paste, hot sauce, broth.
- [ ] 4 — Simmer 20 minutes.

### 3. Prepare Ricotta Filling

- [ ] 1 — Mix ricotta, Italian seasoning, black pepper.

### 4. Assemble Lasagna

- [ ] 1 — Layer meat sauce.
- [ ] 2 — Add pasta sheet.
- [ ] 3 — Add shredded cheese.
- [ ] 4 — Add ricotta.
- [ ] 5 — Repeat layers.
- [ ] 6 — Finish with shredded cheese.

### 5. Bake

- [ ] 1 — Bake 30 minutes at 190°C.
- [ ] 2 — Optional: broil 5–10 minutes for browned top.
- [ ] 3 — Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

## Notes

- The pasta is baked as one slab, then cut into two sheets sized for a 23 × 33 cm (9 × 13 in) baking dish.
- Serves 6–8.
- Total time: ~1 hour 30 minutes.
