# Healthy Egg Custard

> High-protein dessert. Sweetened with monk fruit / erythritol so sugar stays near zero.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷2) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 525          | 263              |
| Protein  | 59 g         | 30 g             |
| Fat      | 28 g         | 14 g             |
| Carbs    | 11 g         | 6 g              |
| Sugar    | 10 g         | 5 g              |

## Ingredients

- [x] 4 eggs
- [ ] 200 ml milk
- [ ] 30 g whey protein
- [ ] 1 tsp gelatin
- [ ] Monk fruit or erythritol sweetener (to taste)
- [ ] Nutmeg (on top)
- [ ] Vanilla (optional)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Warm milk gently in a saucepan.
- [ ] 2 — Dissolve gelatin into the warm milk.
- [ ] 3 — Beat eggs in a separate bowl.
- [ ] 4 — Add whey carefully to the eggs and mix.
- [ ] 5 — Add sweetener.
- [ ] 6 — Combine eggs + whey with milk + gelatin.
- [ ] 7 — Pour into an oven dish.
- [ ] 8 — Sprinkle nutmeg on top.
- [ ] 9 — Bake gently until edges are firm and the centre has a slight wobble.
- [ ] 10 — Cool before eating so it sets properly.

## Notes

- Do not stir once baking begins if setting as custard.
- Centre should not be fully liquid; a slight wobble is right.
- Cooling improves texture significantly.
