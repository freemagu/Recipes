# Cottage Pie

> Classic cottage pie with red-wine beef and buttery mash.

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷5) |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 650 g beef mince
- [ ] 400 ml homemade beef stock
- [ ] 1 large onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 tbsp tomato puree
- [ ] 1 tbsp plain flour
- [ ] 150 ml red wine
- [ ] Fresh thyme (leaves only)
- [ ] Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Black pepper
- [ ] 900 g potatoes, peeled and chopped
- [ ] 115 g butter
- [ ] 125 ml milk
- [ ] 150 g frozen peas

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Boil potatoes in salted water until tender (12–15 min), drain and mash with butter and milk.
- [ ] 2 — Brown beef mince in a pan until well coloured.
- [ ] 3 — Add onion and cook until soft.
- [ ] 4 — Stir in tomato puree and cook 1 minute.
- [ ] 5 — Add flour and cook 1 minute.
- [ ] 6 — Add red wine and reduce slightly.
- [ ] 7 — Add stock and thyme, simmer gently 30–45 minutes until thick.
- [ ] 8 — Season with salt, pepper and Worcestershire sauce.
- [ ] 9 — Transfer meat mixture to baking dish.
- [ ] 10 — Spoon mash over the top.
- [ ] 11 — Grill until golden.
- [ ] 12 — Boil peas, add butter, and serve.

## Notes

- Use homemade beef stock for best results.
