# Mexican Pulled Brisket (Instant Pot)

> Spiced Instant Pot pulled brisket for tacos, lettuce cups, or cauliflower rice.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷6) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 1800         | 300              |
| Protein  | 180 g        | 30 g             |
| Fat      | 95 g         | 16 g             |
| Carbs    | 8 g          | 1 g              |
| Sugar    | 4 g          | 1 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1.0–1.1 kg brisket
- [ ] 1 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 medium onion, sliced
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1 chipotle chilli in adobo (or 1 tsp smoked paprika)
- [ ] 1 tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1 tsp dried oregano
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] ½ tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1½ tsp sea salt
- [ ] 1 tsp ground black pepper
- [ ] 300 ml beef bone broth
- [ ] 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or lime juice
- [ ] 1 tbsp tomato paste (optional)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Press Sauté, heat oil, and brown brisket on all sides (about 5 min). Remove.
- [ ] 2 — Add onion and garlic. Cook ~3 min.
- [ ] 3 — Stir in spices, chipotle, and tomato paste (if using).
- [ ] 4 — Pour in bone broth, scrape up browned bits.
- [ ] 5 — Add vinegar or lime juice.
- [ ] 6 — Return brisket to pot.
- [ ] 7 — Seal lid and pressure cook on High for 90 minutes.
- [ ] 8 — Natural release for 20 minutes.
- [ ] 9 — Remove brisket and shred with forks.
- [ ] 10 — Press Sauté, reduce sauce 10–15 min until thick.
- [ ] 11 — Return shredded beef to sauce and stir through.

## Notes

- Serving suggestions: low-carb tortillas, lettuce cups, or cauliflower rice.
- Toppings: chopped coriander, avocado, red onion, lime.
- Macros above are for the entire batch (toppings/tortillas not included).
