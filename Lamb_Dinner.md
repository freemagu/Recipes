# Lamb Dinner

> A full roast lamb dinner - roast and steaks with potatoes, fennel, honey carrots, and jus.

## Ingredients

### Lamb

- [ ] Lamb steaks (2–4 depending on size)
- [ ] 500 g lamb roast piece
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Black pepper
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] 2–3 garlic cloves
- [ ] Fresh rosemary
- [ ] Butter

### Jus

- [ ] Pan drippings from lamb
- [ ] 1 shallot (finely chopped)
- [ ] Red wine (splash)
- [ ] 300–400 ml beef or lamb stock
- [ ] Butter

### Roast Potatoes

- [ ] 1–1.5 kg potatoes (floury type, e.g. Maris Piper)
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Olive oil or beef dripping/duck fat

### Roast Fennel

- [ ] 2 fennel bulbs
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Black pepper
- [ ] Thyme or lemon zest (optional)

### Honey Carrots

- [ ] 500–700 g carrots
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Small drizzle honey
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Thyme or mint (optional)

## Method

### 1. Prep

- [ ] 1 — Preheat oven to 200°C.
- [ ] 2 — Take lamb out of fridge 30–60 mins before cooking.
- [ ] 3 — Season all lamb generously with salt + pepper.

### 2. Roast Potatoes

- [ ] 1 — Peel and chop potatoes into large chunks.
- [ ] 2 — Boil in salted water for 8–10 mins.
- [ ] 3 — Drain and shake to rough edges.
- [ ] 4 — Put into hot roasting tray with fat.
- [ ] 5 — Roast 40–50 mins, turning once.

### 3. Roast Fennel

- [ ] 1 — Cut fennel into wedges.
- [ ] 2 — Toss with olive oil, salt, pepper.
- [ ] 3 — Roast in oven 25–30 mins until caramelised.

### 4. Lamb Roast Piece

- [ ] 1 — Heat pan with oil until hot.
- [ ] 2 — Sear lamb on all sides until browned.
- [ ] 3 — Transfer to oven.
- [ ] 4 — Roast 15–20 mins (medium-rare target).
- [ ] 5 — Rest 10–15 mins after cooking.

### 5. Lamb Steaks

- [ ] 1 — Heat pan until very hot.
- [ ] 2 — Sear steaks 2–3 mins per side.
- [ ] 3 — Add butter, garlic, rosemary.
- [ ] 4 — Baste for 1 minute.
- [ ] 5 — Rest 5–10 mins.

### 6. Honey Carrots

- [ ] 1 — Peel and cut carrots.
- [ ] 2 — Boil 5–6 mins until just tender.
- [ ] 3 — Drain.
- [ ] 4 — In pan: melt butter + small drizzle honey.
- [ ] 5 — Toss carrots until glossy and coated.
- [ ] 6 — Season with salt.

### 7. Jus

- [ ] 1 — Use lamb pan with drippings.
- [ ] 2 — Add chopped shallot, cook 1–2 mins.
- [ ] 3 — Deglaze with red wine, scrape pan.
- [ ] 4 — Reduce by half.
- [ ] 5 — Add stock, reduce again.
- [ ] 6 — Stir in butter to finish.

### 8. Serve

- [ ] 1 — Slice lamb roast.
- [ ] 2 — Plate steaks whole or sliced.
- [ ] 3 — Add potatoes, fennel, carrots.
- [ ] 4 — Spoon jus over lamb.
