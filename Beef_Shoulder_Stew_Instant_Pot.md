# Beef Shoulder Stew (Instant Pot)

> Hearty Instant Pot beef shoulder stew, portioned for meal prep.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷5) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 4150         | 830              |
| Protein  | 322 g        | 64 g             |
| Fat      | 266 g        | 53 g             |
| Carbs    | 139 g        | 28 g             |
| Sugar    | 42 g         | 8 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1.5 kg beef shoulder, cubed
- [ ] 40 g cooking oil/tallow (beef tallow, ghee, or avocado oil)
- [ ] 300 g onions, diced
- [ ] 150 g celery, chopped
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 50 g tomato paste
- [ ] 250 g mushrooms, quartered
- [ ] 500 g potatoes, cubed
- [ ] 1.5 L bone broth
- [ ] 3 bay leaves
- [ ] 2 tsp dried thyme
- [ ] 2 tsp dried rosemary
- [ ] 2 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] Salt & black pepper to taste
- [ ] Fresh parsley (for garnish)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Prep beef: pat dry, season with salt & pepper.
- [ ] 2 — Sear: Instant Pot Sauté (High), heat oil, sear beef in batches. Remove.
- [ ] 3 — Sauté veg: add onions, celery, garlic. Cook 5 min. Stir in tomato paste + smoked paprika.
- [ ] 4 — Combine: return beef. Add mushrooms, potatoes, bay leaves, thyme, rosemary, bone broth. Stir.
- [ ] 5 — Cook: Pressure Cook (High) for 45 min. Natural release 15 min, then quick release.
- [ ] 6 — Finish: taste, adjust seasoning, thicken if needed.
- [ ] 7 — Garnish with parsley. Divide into 5 containers.

## Notes

- Per portion (5 portions): ~830 kcal, ~64 g protein, ~53 g fat, ~28 g carbs.
- Designed for meal prep.
