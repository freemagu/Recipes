# Guy's Liver Burgers

> Beef-and-liver burger patties - a stealthy way to eat organ meat. Freezer-friendly batch.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1,000 g ground beef
- [ ] 330 g beef liver (blended into a paste)
- [ ] 2 tsp rustic mustard
- [ ] 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 1½ tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 2 tsp onion powder
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] ½ tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1 tsp sweet paprika
- [ ] ½ tsp dried thyme
- [ ] ½ tsp dried oregano
- [ ] ¼ tsp chili flakes or cayenne (optional)
- [ ] 1½ tsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp black pepper (or mild chili powder)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Blend the liver until smooth.
- [ ] 2 — In a large bowl, mix beef, liver, and all seasonings/sauces until fully combined.
- [ ] 3 — If too soft to shape, chill the mixture for 30–60 minutes.
- [ ] 4 — Shape into 10–12 patties (110–130 g each).
- [ ] 5 — Sear in a cast iron pan — 2–3 minutes per side.
- [ ] 6 — Optionally finish in the oven at 180°C for 5–7 minutes (internal temp target: 70°C).

## Notes

- Freeze raw patties with baking paper in between.
- Reheat cooked patties in oven at 160°C for ~10 min.
