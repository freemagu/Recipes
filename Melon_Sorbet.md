# Melon Sorbet

> Smooth melon sorbet - kid-friendly, no alcohol in the mix.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 700 g ripe melon flesh (Cantaloupe, Galia, or Honeydew)
- [ ] 120 g white sugar
- [ ] 100 ml water
- [ ] 1½ tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
- [ ] ¼ tsp guar gum (about 1 g)
- [ ] Pinch of salt

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Heat water and sugar in a saucepan until fully dissolved. Let cool to room temperature.
- [ ] 2 — Remove peel and seeds from melon, cut into chunks. Weigh 700 g of melon flesh.
- [ ] 3 — Combine melon, cooled syrup, lemon juice, salt, and guar gum in a vacuum blender or regular blender. Blend until completely smooth (1–2 minutes).
- [ ] 4 — Refrigerate the mix for at least 2 hours. If using a vacuum blender, leave the mix under vacuum in the jug while chilling.
- [ ] 5 — If using a Magimix-style compressor ice cream maker, pour 2–3 tbsp of 96% food-grade alcohol into the base chamber (not into the sorbet mix). Place the removable bowl on top.
- [ ] 6 — Pour the chilled mix into the ice cream maker and churn for 20–30 minutes.
- [ ] 7 — Transfer to a container and freeze for 2–3 hours to firm up.
- [ ] 8 — Let soften 5–10 minutes before serving if stored at –18°C.

## Notes

- Yield: ~1 litre.
- No alcohol or syrup in the mix — kid-friendly.
