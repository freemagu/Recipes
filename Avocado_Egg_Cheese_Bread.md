# Avocado Egg Cheese Bread

> Low-carb, high-fat bread replacement. Good with meat, eggs, or smoked pork.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷3) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 845          | 282              |
| Protein  | 38 g         | 13 g             |
| Fat      | 72 g         | 24 g             |
| Carbs    | 19 g         | 6 g              |
| Sugar    | 2 g          | 1 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 avocado
- [ ] 1 large egg
- [ ] 1 cup grated cheese

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Mash avocado.
- [ ] 2 — Mix with egg and grated cheese.
- [ ] 3 — Spread into 3 pita-shaped breads on a lined tray.
- [ ] 4 — Bake at 200 °C for ~30 minutes until golden.
- [ ] 5 — Cool slightly before eating.

## Notes

- Low carb, high fat.
- Pairs with bacon, smoked pork, eggs.
- Makes 3 pita-shaped pieces.
