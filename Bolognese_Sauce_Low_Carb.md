# Bolognese Sauce (Low Carb)

> Rich low-carb bolognese with added collagen, made in the Instant Pot.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 500 g minced beef (20% fat)
- [ ] 500 g minced pork
- [ ] 150–200 g diced beef shin or cheek (optional)
- [ ] 2 tbsp butter or 1 tbsp beef tallow
- [ ] ½ small onion, finely diced
- [ ] ½ small carrot, finely diced
- [ ] 1 small celery stick, finely diced
- [ ] 50–100 ml dry red wine (optional)
- [ ] 100 ml heavy cream
- [ ] 1 tbsp tomato purée
- [ ] 200 ml passata or bone broth
- [ ] 250–300 ml beef or chicken stock
- [ ] 1–2 tbsp collagen powder
- [ ] 1 tsp anchovy paste
- [ ] Salt and black pepper

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Set Instant Pot to Sauté (High) and add butter or tallow.
- [ ] 2 — Sauté onion, carrot, and celery for 3–4 minutes.
- [ ] 3 — Add minced beef, pork, and optional beef shin; brown for 5–7 minutes.
- [ ] 4 — Deglaze with red wine (if using) and scrape the bottom; simmer 1 minute.
- [ ] 5 — Add cream, tomato purée, passata or broth, stock, collagen powder, and anchovy paste; stir well.
- [ ] 6 — Seal Instant Pot and cook 35 minutes on High Pressure.
- [ ] 7 — Natural release for 10–15 minutes.
- [ ] 8 — Switch to Sauté (Low) and reduce 5–10 minutes until thickened.
- [ ] 9 — Season with salt and pepper to taste.
