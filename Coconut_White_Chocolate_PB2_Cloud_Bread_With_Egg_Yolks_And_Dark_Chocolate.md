# Coconut White Chocolate PB2 Cloud Bread with Egg Yolks and Dark Chocolate

A richer, more moist version of the original protein cloud bread. This version keeps the 400 g carton of liquid egg whites, adds two separated egg yolks for richness, adds the two extra egg whites into the main egg white base, and folds in 10 g dark chocolate chips.

## Ingredients

- 400 g liquid egg whites (whole carton)
- 2 additional egg whites, separated from whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks, separated from the same whole eggs
- 20 g Vilgain White Chocolate Whey
- 10 g egg white powder
- 10 g PB2 powdered peanut butter
- 10 g dark chocolate chips
- 20 g Lakanto Monk Fruit sweetener
- 1 tsp coconut flavouring
- Pinch of salt

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 140°C.
2. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
3. Separate two whole eggs.
4. Add the two extra egg whites into the 400 g carton of liquid egg whites.
5. Keep the two yolks in a separate small bowl.
6. Add the egg whites and salt to a large bowl.
7. Whip until stiff peaks form.
8. In a separate bowl, mix together:
   - 2 egg yolks
   - 20 g whey
   - 10 g egg white powder
   - 10 g PB2
   - 20 g Lakanto
   - 1 tsp coconut flavouring
9. Fold the yolk mixture gently into the whipped egg whites.
10. Fold in the dark chocolate chips at the very end.
11. Shape into a loaf or dome on the baking paper.
12. Bake for 40-50 minutes.
13. Turn the oven off and leave inside for 15 minutes with the door slightly open.
14. Remove and cool completely before slicing.

## Notes

- The two extra egg whites are included in the macros.
- The yolks make the loaf richer and more moist, but they may slightly reduce the rise compared with the lean version.
- Fold gently after adding the yolk mixture because fat can deflate whipped egg whites.
