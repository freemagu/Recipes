# Keto-Friendly Coconut Gelato with Toasted Coconut Flakes

> Keto coconut gelato with toasted coconut flakes.

## Nutrition

| Macro    | Whole recipe | Per serving (÷4) |
| -------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Calories | 700          | 175              |
| Protein  | 8 g          | 2 g              |
| Fat      | 68 g         | 17 g             |
| Carbs    | 6 g          | 2 g              |
| Sugar    | 8 g          | 2 g              |

## Ingredients

- [ ] ¾ cup (180 ml) full-fat coconut milk
- [ ] ½ cup (120 ml) heavy cream
- [ ] ⅓ cup + 1 tbsp (90 ml) coconut cream
- [ ] ¼ cup (40 g) powdered erythritol
- [ ] 2 large egg yolks
- [ ] ¼ cup (20 g) unsweetened shredded coconut, toasted
- [ ] ¼ tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] ⅛ tsp (0.75 g) salt
- [ ] ¼ tsp (1–2 ml) 96% ethyl alcohol (optional, for texture)
- [ ] 1–2 drops coconut extract (optional)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — In a dry skillet over medium heat, toast 20 g of shredded coconut until golden brown, about 3–4 minutes. Stir constantly to avoid burning. Set aside.
- [ ] 2 — In a medium saucepan, combine 180 ml coconut milk, 120 ml heavy cream, and 90 ml coconut cream with half of the erythritol (20 g). Heat over medium-low until steaming but not boiling.
- [ ] 3 — In a separate bowl, whisk 2 egg yolks with the remaining 20 g erythritol until pale. Slowly add a ladle of hot cream mixture while whisking constantly. Then pour yolk mixture back into saucepan.
- [ ] 4 — Return the pot to medium-low heat and stir constantly with a wooden spoon or spatula until thickened (170–175°F / 77–80°C), about 5–7 minutes. It should coat the back of a spoon.
- [ ] 5 — Strain mixture into a clean bowl to remove any curdles. Stir in 0.75 g salt, ¼ tsp vanilla, 1–2 ml ethyl alcohol (optional), and most of the toasted coconut. Chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
- [ ] 6 — Pour the chilled base into your ice cream maker and churn according to manufacturer's instructions (about 20–30 minutes).
- [ ] 7 — Transfer to a container, sprinkle with the remaining toasted coconut, and freeze for 2–4 hours to firm up before scooping.

## Notes

- Yield: ~500 mL / ~484 g.
- Net carbs: 6 g for the batch.
