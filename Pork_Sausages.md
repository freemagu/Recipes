## Ingredients

- [ ] 400 g minced pork
- [ ] Rosemary
- [ ] Sage
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] Salt (if used)
- [ ] Pepper (if used)

## Method

- [ ] 1 — Mix pork with herbs and egg.
- [ ] 2 — Shape into 6 large sausages.
- [ ] 3 — Cook in pan, oven, or air fryer until fully cooked through.
- [ ] 4 — Serve with eggs, [[Avocado Egg Cheese Bread]], or a low-carb side.
